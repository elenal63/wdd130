Here is All About Me

Who Am I?

Hello everyone! I'm Elena Leon and I'm from Southern California. I live in a little city called Norwalk, which most of you have probably never heard of. But I live just 20 minutes from Disneyland! I am the oldest of three siblings and I am a whole decade older than them, so there's a bit of an age gap. I love singing, painting, going on spontaneous trips with family and friends, and doing anything outdoors. I go on lots of hikes with my boyfriend and we try to play a bunch of intramural sports together throughout the semester. I've been going to church since I was five, because my mom is a convert. I really only missed out on nursery, but as I grew up I remember learning the gospel with my mom and going out with the missionaries so I feel more like a convert. I love going to the temple as much as I can. My favorite scripture is found in Isaiah 53:3.

What Are My Goals

I am currently majoring in Computer Science, but I am switching my major to Web Design and Development. I was in a four year Computer Science Pathway at my high school and I really enjoyed it. I was even a TA for Web Design for two years in high school and it was so rewarding for me to help grades 9-12 learn how to code for the first time. High school really helped me gain my passion for coding and the experiences I had was a big determining factor into deciding my major in college. I hope to graduate with a BA and work at cool tech companies. I really want to make an impact and help others with my work. I have enjoyed my time studying at BYU-I and I love the opportunites we have to feel the Spirit and connect everything back to the gospel. As school and balancing life can be daunting, we can always take a step back and remember to trust in the Lord.
