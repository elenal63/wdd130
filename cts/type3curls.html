Are your curls type 3? You're in the right place! Above are some examples of type 3 curls for reference. The beauty about curls is that regardless of the type, they can look very different on each head of hair. Type 3 curls are typically ringlet curls and can range between loose and tight. If you have type 3 curls, here are some of the most recommended products that you should include in your routine! Be mindful that products work differently on everyone, so it might be trial and error. Hopefully, I can at least put you in the right direction to find products that are made for your hair type.

Steps in a hair routine:

  • Step 1: Shower - Cleanse your hair with Shampoo and Conditioner. Deep Condition your hair once a week. Always detangle in the shower.
  • Step 2 (OPTIONAL): Prep - Right out of shower you can put in Leave In Conditioner to help moisturize your air for the next step.
  • Step 3: Style - Apply styling cream and gel. You will want to use only 2-3 products. Using more on type 3 will weigh your hair down.
  • Step 4: Dry - You can air dry or diffuse. Whichever is most convenient!

Embrace your natural beauty!
