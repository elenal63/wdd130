Steps in a hair routine:

  • Step 1: Shower - Shampoo, Condition, and Deep Condition
  • Step 2 (OPTIONAL): Right out of shower - Leave in conditioner (Helps detangle and moisturize)
  • Step 3: Style - Apply styling cream and gel
  • Step 4: Dry - Air dry or defuse

Embrace your natural beauty!
