Do you have type 4 curls? You're in the right place! Above are some examples of type 4 curls for reference. The beauty about curls is that regardless of the type, they can look very different on each head of hair. Type 4 curls are typically tight and have a lot of shrinkage. If you take a look back at the hair types section on the home page, you can see that type 4 can be considerly more coily. Here are some of the most recommended products that you should include in your routine for type 4! Be mindful that products work differently on everyone, so it might be trial and error. Hopefully, I can at least put you in the right direction to find products that are made for your hair type.

Steps in a hair routine:

  • Step 1: Shower - Shampoo, Condition, and Deep Condition
  • Step 2 (OPTIONAL): Right out of shower - Leave in conditioner (Helps detangle and moisturize)
  • Step 3: Style - Apply styling cream and gel
  • Step 4: Dry - Air dry or defuse

Show off your natural curls!
