Relax, Take A Breather

After the rush you get from rafting all the day, enjoy the gorgeous camp grounds conviently close to the river. Here are our top campgrounds located by famous white water rafting sites. Enjoy the trip with family and friends.

Hells Gate State Park (ID)

This campground is located by the Snake River. The river goes through Hells Canyon, which is the deepest gorge in the United States! Staying at Hells Gate provides easy access to surrounding rafting companies. The campground is pretty large and includes flushed toilets, showers, water, and electricity.

Book Now

Valley of the Rogue State Park (OR)

Located along the Rogue River, this campground has full-hookup RV sites, flushed toilets, water, electricity, and eight yurts available to rent. The Rogue River is one of the most wild and beautiful scenic rivers with high ranked river rapids.

Book Now

Skidaway Island State Park (GA)

This gorgeous campground is located right by the Savannah River, which is popular for the relazing friendly environment it brings for tubing and floating. The large campground has 87 tent, trailer, amd RV sites. It also offers flushed toilets, showers, water, and electricity.
